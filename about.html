About Us

Code-Inspired Cuisine and Beverages

GitPub opened in the summer of 2021, aimed at providing an innovative spin on the traditional Irish Pub. The owners started with a passion for web development and combined their love of coding with their gift of hospitality.

Come enjoy fantastic code-themed dishes like our CSScargot or the famous Char-grilled steak. There's fun for the whole family and children will devour the fish and scripts faster than you can say “hosting provider.” Our executive chef, Brandi Boolean, is always coming up with fresh and amazing dishes for you to try.

Thank you to all the wonderful friends, old and new, and to all the employees that have helped us become the premiere destination for coders and foodies alike. Sláinte!
